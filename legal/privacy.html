Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our services or contact us, we may collect:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone number)
  • Company name and business information
  • Website or social media handles
  • Communication preferences
  • Service requirements and project details

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you visit our website:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referral sources

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve website functionality
  • Analyze website usage
  • Personalize your experience
  • Track marketing campaign effectiveness

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver AI growth solutions and support
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries and provide updates
  • Improvement: Enhance our services and website experience
  • Marketing: Send relevant information about our services (with consent)
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal and regulatory requirements

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data based on:

  • Consent you have given
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate business interests
  • Legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

Service Providers

  • Cloud hosting providers
  • Email marketing platforms
  • Analytics services
  • Payment processors

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety
  • Investigate fraud or security issues

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures:

  • Encryption: Data encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access on need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and updates
  • Secure Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade hosting and backup
  • Staff Training: Regular security awareness training

While we use industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We continuously monitor and improve our security practices.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your data
  • Objection: Object to processing for marketing purposes
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@yourbrand.com

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

Specific retention periods:

  • Contact Information: 3 years after last contact
  • Project Data: 5 years after project completion
  • Marketing Data: Until consent is withdrawn
  • Website Analytics: 26 months

7. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside India. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Adequacy decisions
  • Standard contractual clauses
  • Certification schemes
  • Binding corporate rules

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website
  • Update the "Last updated" date
  • Notify you of significant changes via email
  • Obtain consent for material changes where required
