Data Processing Agreement

How we process and protect your data in compliance with privacy regulations

Last updated: January 2025

1. Parties and Definitions

Parties

  • Data Controller: You (the client) who determines the purposes and means of processing personal data
  • Data Processor: AI Growth Studio, who processes personal data on behalf of the controller

Key Definitions

  • Personal Data: Any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person
  • Processing: Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, use, and deletion
  • Data Subject: The individual whose personal data is being processed
  • GDPR: General Data Protection Regulation (EU) 2016/679

2. Scope and Purpose

This Data Processing Agreement (DPA) governs the processing of personal data by AI Growth Studio on behalf of clients in connection with our services:

  • AI video creation and content production
  • Social media automation and management
  • Website development and hosting
  • AI operations tools and workflows
  • Customer support and communication

This agreement supplements our main service agreement and privacy policy.

3. Data Processing Details

Categories of Data Subjects

  • Client employees and representatives
  • Client customers and prospects
  • Website visitors and users
  • Social media followers and engagers

Categories of Personal Data

  • Contact information (names, emails, phone numbers)
  • Professional information (job titles, company details)
  • Communication data (messages, inquiries, responses)
  • Usage data (website analytics, engagement metrics)
  • Technical data (IP addresses, device information)

Processing Activities

  • Collection and storage of data
  • Content creation and customization
  • Automated responses and communications
  • Analytics and reporting
  • System maintenance and support

4. Data Processor Obligations

AI Growth Studio commits to:

Processing Instructions

  • Process personal data only on documented instructions from the client
  • Not process data for our own purposes
  • Immediately inform client if instructions violate applicable law

Confidentiality

  • Ensure all personnel processing data are bound by confidentiality
  • Provide appropriate training on data protection
  • Limit access to data on a need-to-know basis

Security Measures

  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures
  • Ensure data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Incident response and breach notification procedures

5. Sub-processors

We may engage sub-processors to assist in providing services. Current sub-processors include:

Infrastructure Providers

  • Cloud Hosting: AWS, Google Cloud Platform
  • CDN Services: Cloudflare
  • Email Services: SendGrid, Mailchimp

Service Providers

  • Analytics: Google Analytics
  • Customer Support: Intercom, Zendesk
  • Payment Processing: Stripe, Razorpay

We will:

  • Maintain an updated list of sub-processors
  • Ensure all sub-processors meet equivalent data protection standards
  • Notify clients of any changes to sub-processors
  • Allow clients to object to new sub-processors

6. Data Subject Rights

We will assist clients in fulfilling data subject rights requests:

Rights Supported

  • Access: Provide copies of personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Delete data when legally required
  • Portability: Export data in structured format
  • Restriction: Limit processing activities
  • Objection: Stop processing for specific purposes

Response Process

  • Forward requests to client within 24 hours
  • Provide technical assistance in responding
  • Implement requested changes within agreed timeframes
  • Document all actions taken

7. Data Transfers

Personal data may be transferred to countries outside the EEA/India. We ensure adequate protection through:

Transfer Mechanisms

  • European Commission adequacy decisions
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs)
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct

Safeguards

  • Regular assessment of transfer mechanisms
  • Additional security measures where required
  • Documentation of all international transfers
  • Client notification of any changes

8. Data Breach Notification

Notification Timeline

  • Internal Detection: Immediate internal escalation
  • Client Notification: Within 24 hours of discovery
  • Regulatory Notification: Client responsibility with our assistance
  • Data Subject Notification: As required by law

Breach Response

  • Immediate containment and assessment
  • Detailed incident documentation
  • Root cause analysis and remediation
  • Prevention measures implementation

Information Provided

  • Nature and scope of the breach
  • Categories and number of affected data subjects
  • Likely consequences and mitigation measures
  • Contact information for further details

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Retention Periods

  • Active Services: Duration of service agreement
  • Post-Termination: 30 days for data retrieval
  • Legal Requirements: As mandated by applicable law
  • Backup Systems: Up to 90 days for disaster recovery

Deletion Process

  • Secure deletion using industry-standard methods
  • Verification of complete data removal
  • Certificate of deletion upon request
  • Documentation of deletion activities

10. Audits and Compliance

Audit Rights

Clients have the right to:

  • Request information about our data processing activities
  • Conduct audits with reasonable notice
  • Review security certifications and assessments
  • Inspect relevant documentation and records

Compliance Monitoring

  • Regular internal compliance assessments
  • Third-party security certifications
  • Staff training and awareness programs
  • Continuous improvement of processes

11. Liability and Indemnification

Liability Allocation

  • Each party liable for damages caused by their breach
  • Joint liability for damages caused by joint processing
  • Limitation of liability as specified in main service agreement

Indemnification

We will indemnify clients against claims arising from our breach of this DPA, subject to:

  • Prompt notification of claims
  • Cooperation in defense
  • Our control over defense and settlement

12. Term and Termination

Duration

This DPA remains in effect for the duration of the main service agreement and any period during which we process personal data on your behalf.

Termination Effects

Upon termination:

  • Cease all processing activities
  • Return or delete all personal data
  • Provide confirmation of data deletion
  • Maintain confidentiality obligations
